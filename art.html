Apel Retirement Trust

This is a Trust which affords the Client the opportunity to plan for retirement alongside the statutorily required pension plan. It offers the choice and flexibility of building a portfolio of large investible funds that will be optimized through effective proactive management.

Benefits
Regular and flexible withdrawal at retirement to meet needs

High return on investment.
Fund is inaccessible to Probate or Creditors.
